Our Noble Mission

Under the stalwart leadership of Commander Ken, we brave the elements to rescue, rehabilitate, and release displaced beach umbrellas throughout the Byron Hinterland.

Every year, countless beach umbrellas fall victim to sudden coastal squalls and freaky storms. We're here to give these wayward sun shields a second chance at life.
